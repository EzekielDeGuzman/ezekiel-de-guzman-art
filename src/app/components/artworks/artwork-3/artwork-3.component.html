One Word From You
28cm x 60cm

Like the others, the title of Figure 8 are lyrics from Mitski’s songs. The line continues: “One word from you… and I would jump off of this ledge I'm on. Tell me ‘don't’ so I can crawl back in.” This piece represents the suffocating pressure to conform to others' expectations, a creative constraint that undermines my artistic voice. I'm perpetually caught between pursuing my genuine vision and catering to external validation, and I find myself trapped in a cycle of self-doubt and self-compromise where I have to sacrifice my creative freedom just to meet the expectations of others.
